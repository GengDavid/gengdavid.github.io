## About Me 
I am a graduate student in [School of Data and Computer Science](http://sdcs.sysu.edu.cn/) at [Sun Yat-Sen university](http://www.sysu.edu.cn/2012/en/index.htm). Currently I'm working with Prof. [Xiaodan Liang](https://lemondan.github.io/) in Sun Yat-sen University. Previously, I've worked with Prof. [Zhuo Su](https://suzhuoi.github.io).  
My research interests lie in the field of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. I'm now researching on the area of scene understanding and AutoML for deep learning.
